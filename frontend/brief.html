The Charter

  1. Every number carries its source and its timestamp. No orphan claims.
  2. No fabricated or interpolated data without explicit marking. Reconstructions are labeled reconstructions.
  3. Gaps are shown, not hidden. A silent source is displayed as silent.
  4. The record is permanent. What we showed on any date is replayable; corrections add to history, they don't erase it.
  5. Raw is one click away. Any rendered value traces to the cached payload and the upstream URL.
